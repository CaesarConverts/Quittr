Account Deletion Request for NoFap: Ranked

This page provides information about how to request the deletion of your account from NoFap: Ranked, developed by Quittr LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have control over your personal data.

How to Request Account or Data Deletion

  1. Choose your request type below (account deletion or data deletion only).
  2. Fill out the form with your email address and username associated with your NoFap: Ranked account.
  3. Submit the form to send your request to our support team.
  4. Check your email for a confirmation message from our team.
  5. Respond to any verification requests to confirm your identity and complete the process.

Our team will process your request within 30 days as per data protection regulations.

Data Deletion Information

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (email address, username)
  • Your profile data and settings
  • Your habit tracking data and progress
  • Your achievement records and statistics
  • Your app preferences and customizations

Data Retention Period

After you submit a deletion request:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately (if requesting account deletion)
  • Your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytical purposes but will not be linked to your identity

Data That May Be Retained

For legal and security purposes, we may retain:

  • Transaction records (for financial compliance)
  • Logs of your deletion request (to prevent fraud)
  • Any data required by law to be retained
